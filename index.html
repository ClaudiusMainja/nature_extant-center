Who We Are As NELC

Nature Extant is dedicated to educating and empowering the next generation of animal law advocates. Our comprehensive programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare you for a successful career in wildlife law and policy. With expert faculty and a global network, we're committed to making a lasting impact on animal welfare and conservation.
